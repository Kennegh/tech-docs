# Thelio Mega (External Overview)

## Back side overview:

![Back Ports](./img/ports-back.webp)

### Audio Port Guide:

- Top row, left to right:
  - Optical S/PDIF Out (square)
  - Rear Speaker Out
  - Center/Subwoofer Out
- Bottom row, left to right:
  - Mic in
  - Line Out / Front Speaker Out
  - Line In / Side Speaker Out

### Power Switch Guide:

- 1: On
- 0: Off
